On 10 July 2017, militants from Lashkar-e-Taiba fired upon a bus carrying Amarnath Yatra pilgrims on the Anantnag-Srinagar highway in Jammu and Kashmir, India. Seven Hindu pilgrims were killed and 19 were injured in the attack. The bus was returning from the annual pilgrimage to the Amarnath cave shrine. Indian security forces launched a massive search operation in the area following the incident.
