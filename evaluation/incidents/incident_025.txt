On 5 April 2020, two Hizbul Mujahideen militants attacked a Border Security Force patrol near Handwara in Kupwara district of Jammu and Kashmir, India. The militants opened fire on the BSF vehicle, killing 3 BSF jawans and injuring 2 others. The attackers fled into a nearby forested area. Security forces launched a cordon and search operation in the surrounding villages.
