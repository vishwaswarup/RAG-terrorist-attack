On 27 March 2016, a suicide bomber detonated an explosive device in Gulshan-e-Iqbal Park in Lahore, Pakistan, on Easter Sunday. The park was crowded with families and children celebrating the holiday. Jamaat-ul-Ahrar, a faction of the Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan, claimed responsibility for the attack. At least 75 people were killed and more than 340 were injured in the blast.
